The Perfect Taco

Description

This taco recipe is simple but tasty.

Ingredients

  • corn tortilla
  • shredded chicken
  • beans
  • lettuce
  • tomato
  • sour cream

Directions

  1. get the tortilla ready to get filled
  2. grill the chicken
  3. add the canned beans
  4. add the sour cream
  5. add the lettuce and tomato
  6. enjoy!
